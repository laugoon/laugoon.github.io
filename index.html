About Me

I got the MEng degree in Computer Technology from North China Electric Power University in 2024. At the Information Security Laboratory, I am working under the guidance of Prof. Ying Zhang, with additional mentorship from Dr. Xiang Hu and Dr. Xuena Zhang. My research focuses on Transfer Learning and Explainable Artificial Intelligence(Explainable AI), particularly their applications in Medicine.

Currently, I am also interning at EIT, collaborating with Dr. Xiaoyu Shen on projects related to large language models (LLMs) and information extraction from long documents.

I am actively seeking a funded Ph.D. position. Feel free to connect with me on WeChat or via email.

Research

During my graduate studies, I conducted research under Prof. Ying Zhang's supervision. We proposed a framework to increase the accuracy of computer-aided diagnosis of complications after liver transplantation with transfer learning, and to explore key features of postoperative complications. Now, our work is accepted by Interdisciplinary Sciences: Computational Life Sciences(INSC, JCR: MATHMATICAL & COMPUTATIONAL BIOLOGY Q1). [Link] [Code]
